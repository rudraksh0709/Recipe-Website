Crispy Masala Dosa

⏱ Time: 20 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Dosa Batter (fermented)
  • 3 boiled and mashed Potatoes
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds & Curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • Oil or Ghee for roasting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the potato masala by tempering mustard seeds and curry leaves, then mixing with potatoes and turmeric.
  2. Heat a flat tawa (griddle) and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thinly in a circular motion.
  3. Drizzle oil on the edges and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Place a portion of the potato filling in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot.
