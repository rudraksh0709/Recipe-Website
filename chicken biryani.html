Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

⏱ Time: 60 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • 500g Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken
  • 1 cup Fried Onions
  • Saffron and Whole Spices

Instructions

  1. Par-boil rice with whole spices.
  2. Marinate chicken and place at pot bottom.
  3. Layer rice over chicken and add saffron.
  4. Cook on low heat (Dum) for 30 minutes.
