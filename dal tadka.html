Homestyle Dal Tadka

The ultimate soul food: yellow lentils with a smoky garlic temper.

⏱ Time: 25 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Easy

The Pantry List

  • 1 cup Arhar/Toor Dal (Pigeon Peas)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds & 4 cloves Garlic
  • 2 Dried Red Chilies
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

The Cooking Process

  1. Pressure cook the dal with turmeric and salt until soft.
  2. In a small pan, heat ghee until it shimmers.
  3. Add cumin, dried chilies, and minced garlic until they turn golden brown.
  4. Pour this 'Tadka' (tempering) directly over the cooked dal and cover immediately.
