Tandoori Paneer Tikka

⏱ Time: 30 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 400g Paneer cubes
  • 1 cup Thick Yogurt
  • 1 Bell Pepper & 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsp Tikka Masala

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt and spices to create a marinade.
  2. Coat paneer and veggies in the mix for 20 mins.
  3. Skewer them and grill until charred at the edges.
