Butter Chicken

⏱ Time: 45 Mins 🔥 Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken Thighs
  • 2 cups Tomato Puree
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • Whole Spices

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in spices and yogurt.
  2. Sauté the tomato puree with butter and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add chicken and simmer until tender.
  4. Finish with heavy cream and dried fenugreek.
